Welcome to Study of Life

Study of Life (or as we like to say, "SoL") is an organization dedicated to biology. That's it. Biology. Whether you don't quite understand a concept from class, or want help on tests like the Biology SAT II's and the AP Biology test, or even want to learn about what opportunities are out there, we are here to help.

Between us (Yilun and Varun), we've participated in a variety of biology-related competitions including Science Olympiad, Science Bowl, and the USA Biology Olympiad. We've had amazing experiences and want to encourage students to participate and spread awareness of these opportunities.

Check out our blog for study tips, cool opportunities, and bonus problems!

Visit our resource center for advice, problem sets, and handouts!
