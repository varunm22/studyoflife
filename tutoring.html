Signup

Click here to sign up for an informational Skype session. During this 5-10 minute meeting, we will discuss your specific goals and plan logistics regarding meeting times and payment. Having this meeting does not bind you to tutoring. We encourage you to sign up if you are unsure about starting tutoring and simply want to find out what exactly it would entail for you.

 

Structure

  • 1 or 2 lessons a week via Skype with Yilun, Varun, or another qualified tutor

  • Payment is through Venmo or Paypal

  • Discussions/lectures on assigned reading

  • Discussions/lectures on assigned handouts and problem sets

Types

  • General lessons - between lessons, students read assigned chapters and work on problems. During lessons, tutors discuss concepts with the students 1 on 1. This plan can be adapted to fit the student's time commitment and experience. These lessons will continue through the year. Price: $30 per hour.

  • Crash course - these lessons will be held before large events including the AP Biology exam and the USA Biology exams. Students will be taught just the most relevant topics in a group setting. These lessons will be announced on the website's home page. Price: Contact an Instructor.

  • Selected topics - students having difficulty with select topics or units can sign up for just one lesson at a time when they need explanations, such as before a unit test in school. However, students signing up for these lessons will be given lower priority in scheduling to students taking general lessons. Price: $30 per hour.

Expectations

  • Students should read the assigned chapters and reread portions they don't understand. The faster the tutors can get through the assigned material, the more time they have to teach advanced topics and help connect the material to other concepts in biology for deeper understanding.

  • Students should independently work on the assigned problem sets throughout the units. If the students have not attempted the problems prior to problem set-focused lessons, the tutors will be less able to teach problem solving skills and specialized knowledge.

  • Students should plan or confirm lecture times with their tutors at least 72 hours in advance.

  • Students who must cancel lectures should do so at least 24 hours before the lecture.
